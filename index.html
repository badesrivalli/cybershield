About Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks and data from cyber attacks. It helps individuals and organizations stay safe from hackers, malware, ransomware and phishing attacks.

Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Fake emails or websites designed to steal your personal information.

Malware

Malicious software that damages computers or steals sensitive data.

Password Attack

Weak passwords can be guessed or cracked by attackers.

Ransomware

Blocks access to files until money is paid to the attacker.
